# Business Sales Performance Analytics

**Internship:** Data Science & Analytics – Task 1

This project analyzes online retail sales data to generate insights on revenue trends, top products, country performance, and customer demand. 

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

## Project Highlights
- Cleaned and organized raw sales data
- Analyzed revenue trends over time
- Identified top-selling products and countries
- Created charts and dashboards
- Provided actionable business recommendations

## Key Insights
- Revenue peaks during holiday seasons
- Top products contribute the most to overall revenue
- United Kingdom has the highest sales
- Frequent products are important to maintain consistent sales
